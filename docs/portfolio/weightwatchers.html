WeightWatchers: Elevating Member Outcomes Through Experience Design

Consumer App Design & Strategy

The Challenge

WeightWatchers faced a critical issue: members were abandoning the program due to outdated digital offerings. The rise of competitors like Fitbit and MyFitnessPal underscored the need for a modernized digital experience. Millions of members still engaged with in-person meetings and weight management programs, but a lack of integration with modern technology left them vulnerable to churn.

My Role

As Lead Experience Designer, I worked alongside designers, product managers, engineers, and health experts from both Slalom and WeightWatchers to envision and execute a comprehensive cross-platform solution. This included defining user journeys, creating design systems, and ensuring cohesive execution across all teams.

Development

Rebuilding the Digital Experience: We prioritized a user-centered approach to integrate fitness tracking, food logging, and community engagement seamlessly. The first milestone was fast-tracking fitness tracker integration, enabling members to connect devices like Fitbit and Withings to their WeightWatchers account.

Enhancing Food Tracking: Food tracking was redesigned for ease and engagement. We introduced new features such as favorites, recipes, and a health dashboard to give members actionable insights. Design principles like clarity and positive reinforcement ensured high usability, mitigating the risk of user drop-off due to the high-touch nature of the app.

Empathy-Driven Design: I immersed myself in the WeightWatchers ecosystem, joining as a member and testing both existing and competitive solutions. This hands-on approach informed design decisions with authentic user perspectives. Participating in user research and testing helped validate our designs, ensuring they met real-world needs.

Outcomes

The Beyond the Scale platform launched successfully ahead of the crucial holiday season. It transformed WeightWatchers' digital offerings into a competitive asset. Key outcomes included increased membership engagement, a revitalized digital ecosystem, and a stronger market position. Additionally, the company attracted notable investments, including Oprah's high-profile involvement.

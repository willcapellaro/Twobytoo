WeightWatchers: Elevating Member Outcomes Through Experience Design

Consumer App Design & Strategy

The Challenge

WeightWatchers faced a critical issue: members were abandoning the program due to outdated digital offerings. The rise of competitors like Fitbit and MyFitnessPal underscored the need for a modernized digital experience. Millions of members still engaged with in-person meetings and weight management programs, but a lack of integration with modern technology left them vulnerable to churn.

My Role

As Lead Experience Designer, I worked alongside designers, product managers, engineers, and health experts from both Slalom and WeightWatchers to envision and execute a comprehensive cross-platform solution. This included defining user journeys, creating design systems, and ensuring cohesive execution across all teams. I also contributed detailed screen architecture diagrams to ensure clarity and alignment across the project.

Development

Phase 0: Wearable activation prototype

Phase 0: Fitness Integration: We fast-tracked an app enabling fitness tracker integration, addressing the most urgent need. Members could connect devices like Fitbit and Withings to their WeightWatchers account, eliminating the need to switch between apps. The backend portal supported syncing and offered a dashboard showing fitness activity, rewarding users with tangible benefits such as dietary allowances based on activity.

Research & Empathy

User Research: In-person session at WeightWatchers lab

I immersed myself in the WeightWatchers ecosystem, joining as a member and testing both existing and competitive solutions. This hands-on approach informed design decisions with authentic user perspectives. In addition to joining as a member, I conducted in-person and digital research sessions, gathering feedback to refine designs and prioritize features effectively.

Outcomes

The Beyond the Scale platform launched successfully ahead of the crucial holiday season. It transformed WeightWatchers' digital offerings into a competitive asset. Key outcomes included increased membership engagement, a revitalized digital ecosystem, and a stronger market position. Additionally, the company attracted notable investments, including Oprah's high-profile involvement.

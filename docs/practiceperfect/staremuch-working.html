What is the STARE Framework?

The STARE framework is a structured way to ace behavioral interviews. Each letter stands for a key step that showcases your skills, impact, and ability to learn from experiences.

Situation

Set the stage by describing the context of the challenge.

Task

Explain the specific goal or responsibility you handled.

Action

Highlight the steps you took to tackle the problem.

Result

Share the measurable outcomes of your effort.

Evaluation/Enhancement

Reflect on how you grew or adapted from the experience. What will you do differently in the future?
